Vyacheslav Munister is a researcher in the field of vehicle informatization of technological processes occurring in wireless and unmanned computer systems.

At one time, he published scientific works devoted to the implementation of fuzzy control tools and econometric modeling.

His works have practical implementations in the form of registered and copyrighted software.

Areas of interest: Internet of things, andragogy (non scholae, sed vitae discimus), fuzzy modeling and control; information law; means of identification, measurement, transmission and processing in IoT, IoE, aspects and problems of integration of microprocessor systems within the framework of the concept of “ambient intelligence”

The scope of attention includes the study of tools of economic planning, town planning and urbanism, expert systems, logistics, the theory of economic mechanisms, relational contraction.

The author has carried out work on the selection, classification (ranking), systematization and formalization of the topic of organizing the functioning of digital transport ecosystems as a new phenomenon in cybernetic.

The importance of the role of differentiation of ecosystems into layers (levels) is substantiated: key conclusions on the comprehensive study of this topic are demonstrated

The goal was also to create a generalized characteristics of the organization of transport logistics of the enterprise in the context of the transformation of digital economy
